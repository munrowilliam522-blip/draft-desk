## Trait Ratings

**Trait 1 — Accuracy (4/10)**

Accuracy on short and sideline throws against Auburn was poor. Good portion of completed throws are behind LOS and inside the numbers. Not good under pressure. Some balls towards the sidelines just turn into 50/50 lob balls.

**Trait 2 — Vision (6/10)**

Good job going through reads. Can take an extra second to make the decisive decision. Keeps eyes downfield. Can freeze and turn into a statue if nothing is open.

**Trait 3 — Arm Strength / Velocity (4/5)**

Passes get to receiver on time. Ball zips through the air, doesn't really float. Didn't get to see many deep balls.

**Trait 4 — Pocket Presence / Escapability (3/5)**

Sometimes doesn't see the pocket collapsing around him. Should step up more. Once he diagnoses the issue, can use athleticism to escape. Has athleticism to escape, but loses track of the rushers around him. Will not create a play out of structure.

**Trait 5 — Anticipation (3/5)**

Knows where his receivers are supposed to be and can rotate between the options.

**Trait 6 — Mechanics (3.5/4)**

Fluid motion that looks effortless to throw with. Pre-snap routine is solid.

## Summary

Robertson can operate inside a very structured scheme that creates open short-medium routes. Deeper balls and plays where he has to create something are his weakness. On the run and under pressure, accuracy drops a lot.
