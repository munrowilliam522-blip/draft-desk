## Trait Ratings

**Trait 1 — Accuracy (8/10)**

Does a good job of placing the ball away from defenders on short passes. Good throws on the run. Will place the ball on receivers on the large majority of deep throws; percentage does not represent that.

**Trait 2 — Vision (6/10)**

Can predetermine sometimes which leads to missing of open receivers. Vision is not the best as he is not forced to go through multiple reads. Lots of routes are schemed with one route. Lots of PA boots and roll outs. Most throws are coming between the 0-10 yard line.

**Trait 3 — Arm Strength / Velocity (3/5)**

Okay arm, ball gets to where it needs to go in desired time.

**Trait 4 — Pocket Presence / Escapability (4/5)**

Above average speed and escapability, has a solid amount of designed runs in the playbook. Good feel of the pocket pressure. Sometimes will commit to scrambling and not look up for open receivers as he steps up in the pocket.

**Trait 5 — Anticipation (3/5)**

Isn't forced to anticipate that often. Will recognize where his receivers are supposed to be. Won't really create anything out of structure.

**Trait 6 — Mechanics (3/4)**

Solid mechanics.

## Summary

Klubnik came off a really strong junior season to a lackluster senior season. His strengths come in accuracy and his athleticism. He was not asked to go through that many reads on offense. Honestly, he has the tools to develop into a starting quarterback, but will need to demonstrate his IQ more.
