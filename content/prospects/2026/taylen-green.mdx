## Trait Ratings

**Trait 1 — Accuracy (7/10)**

Very good accuracy when on the run or getting hit. Willing to stand up to the hit and still make an accurate throw. Will sometimes force plays that don't need to happen. Often looking to make something happen even if nothing is available. Can miss high or low on medium-short routes from time to time.

**Trait 2 — Vision (6/10)**

First instinct can be to run when pressure comes. When he has time can go through his reads. Struggles when pressure comes. Will stare down routes he thinks will become open.

**Trait 3 — Arm Strength / Velocity (4.5/5)**

Good arm strength, can really throw it deep and high, mostly due to his height. Needs to work on getting that zip to his shorter route targets.

**Trait 4 — Pocket Presence / Escapability (4/5)**

Really fast straight line runner. Not as quick as he thinks he is. Will underestimate defensive line and get sacked running backwards.

**Trait 5 — Anticipation (3/5)**

Haven't seen exceptional leading of the pass, does what is needed. Needs time to get through his reads.

**Trait 6 — Mechanics (3/4)**

Throwing motion looks a bit jerky. Sort of looks like wild horse in the pocket.

## Summary

Green has a unique build for a quarterback, tall and skinny and very fast. He needs to work on his accuracies and reading of route development, but there will probably be a team out there looking to take a shot on his athletic traits.
